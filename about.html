WHO WE ARE

Theresa Organization is a non-profit organization that brings care to orphaned and vulnerable children around the world.

We empower children, youth, and families to effectively navigate through change, raise healthy children, develop strong relationships, and heal from trauma. We offer regular,
consistent support to those in need so that the children in our care can develop over time, giving them the chance to create a brighter future.

Theresa Organization has opened programs for orphans children, families and communities . We provide youth and family programs which include family foster care, adoption, foster care prevention through family strengthening, mental and behavioral health, parenting supports, afterschool, and youth development. Theresa Organization employs approximately 280 full-time and 150 part-time/on-call staff and manages over 300 volunteers and supporters who come together to help orphan children each year.


OUR MISSION


Theresa Organisation exists to provide care to orphans, vulnerable children, powerless young-women, and broken families. We provide many different types of services to the communities we serve. At Theresa Orphans, we take a long-term approach to what we do and go beyond simple involvement in the lives of the children and people we serve.
