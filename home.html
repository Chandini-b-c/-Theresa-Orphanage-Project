Theresa Orphanage - NGO for Children

Not everyone is blessed with necessary amenities. Though it's a weird law of nature,the only thing we can do is take things into our own hands. We work on providing the most fundamental amenities any human must be getting.We provide shelter to orphan kids who were dejected by their parents.We take care of their shelter,food,medicines and other necessities so they get through their life conveniently. Regular health check ups are done. We also conduct frequent meditation,prayer and yoga sessions for the kids to keep themselves lively and active. We induce hope in the lives of those who would have lost all hopes! We even organise religious trips to close by places!
